# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Students and ML guild members who built a linear regression model and want to explore its predictions interactively. They understand the model — they need a tool that makes the results legible and credible, not a product that sells them.

## Product Purpose

A data exploration tool for a linear regression model trained on international football results. Lets users predict match outcomes, inspect model coefficients, simulate the 2026 World Cup bracket, and understand why the model predicts what it does. Success means a judge or teammate can open it and immediately understand the model's behaviour.

## Brand Personality

Analytical. Honest. Sparse.

## Anti-references

- Neon gaming dashboards (dark background + saturated green/red/blue everything)
- SaaS metric templates (big number, small label, gradient accent)
- Sports broadcast overlays (loud, high-contrast, designed to shout)

## Design Principles

1. Data earns trust through restraint, not decoration.
2. Every color carries meaning — no decorative color.
3. The model is the product; the UI is the frame. Don't compete.
4. Hierarchy through weight and whitespace, not through size explosion.
5. Bracket and charts should read at a glance — annotation over animation.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Body text ≥4.5:1 against background. State colors must not rely on hue alone — pair with label or icon.
